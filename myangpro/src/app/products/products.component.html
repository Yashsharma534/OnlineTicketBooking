When do bookings show up on Cineplex for upcoming movies at a particular cinema?

Cinemas open bookings 2-3 days prior to the release date and this can vary from cinema to cinema. Once bookings go live by the cinema, you can grab your desired tickets and enjoy the show.

My previous transaction was canceled/incomplete. Why do the selected seats appear to be booked?

In case you've selected the seats they are blocked to prevent another user from opting for the same seats. If the booking is left incomplete , the seats are blocked for 7 to 8 minutes which gets unblocked post the mentioned time.

Is there a cut off time for booking the tickets?

Online booking is ideally available 1 hour to 15 minutes prior to the showtime and it varies from cinema to cinema. Certain cinema do allow last minutes booking too :) P.S: Cinema can stop the online booking before the cut off time since they reserve the seats for offline bookings.
